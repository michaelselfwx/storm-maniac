Project 1: Interactive SPC Outlook & Local Storm Reports Map

Using the ArcGIS API, I have created a very user-friendly map of the most recent SPC Day 1, 2, and 3 Convective Outlook as well as all the Local Storm Reports within the last 24 hours. Users can add their own reports directly on the page.

View Project

Project 2: Weather Balloon Radiosonde Tracking

Using simple software-defined radios (SDRs), I am able to track the launch of weather balloons from all over Florida. With one receiver in the Orlando area and multiple in Gainesville, I have a large footprint of Florida within reach of my hand-built antennas.
